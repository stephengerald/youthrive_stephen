Mission

The purpose of LaborX is to facilitate the creation of a truly global, freelance jobs market, that will power the growth of the gig
economy and drive mass cryptocurrency adoption.

LaborX achieves this by removing the barriers inherent in conventional freelance work platforms. Digital work contracts formalise the
agreement between customer and freelancer, with escrowed payments and automatic settlement in cryptocurrency once key milestones are
completed. Intuitive search and a decentralised scoring system enable users to work together with confidence, and a dispute resolution
mechanism ensures fast and cost-effective arbitration of disputes if necessary.

History

Chrono.tech, an Australian blockchain company, launched LaborX on May 10, 2024. This platform serves as an on-demand work marketplace,
connecting freelancers and clients through smart contracts on the Ethereum network.

LaborX leverages blockchain technology to provide secure and efficient payments, as well as robust protections for both parties involved
in freelance work.By using smart contracts, LaborX ensures transparency, trust, and fair compensation for freelancers while enabling
clients to find skilled professionals for their projects.The platform aims to revolutionize the gig economy by eliminating intermediaries
and streamlining the hiring process.
